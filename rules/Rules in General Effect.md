
# Rules in General Effect

## Broken Morale

If at any time the number of Alive models in a party falls below half of their original number, Broken Morale is triggered. The models of that party become affected by the **Panicked State** until the end of the game. (If the number of models rises above half again, this rule ceases to be in effect.)

## Friendly Fire

If a model delivers a Hit that could deal damage to an allied model, that allied model must perform a Morale roll. On failure, that allied model becomes **Panicked** until its next Strategic Phase.

## Priority Over the Core Rules

Abilities (Skills, Traits, Combat Arts, Spellcrafts), Spells, Items, Upgrades, Class-specific Abilities, Stratagems, and certain Effects take priority over the core rules and may override their restrictions. Rules stated on cards or within Spells take precedence.

## End of the Game

- The Quest automatically ends if, at the beginning of their turn, a player has all models in either the Incapacitated or Dead State.
- Unless specified otherwise, the game ends after the last player concludes their 5th turn.
- The victorious player is the player who scored the greater amount of **Victory Points (VP)**.
- If all players have the same amount of VP, the game ends in a draw.
- Unless stated otherwise, the maximum number of VP a player can obtain in a single Quest is 10.

## Recovery Check

During its own Strategic Phase, an Incapacitated model may perform a T roll:

- **On success:** The model regains 1 HP and gains the Weakened State.
- **On failure:** The model remains Incapacitated.

An Incapacitated model with an allied model in Base Contact (where neither model is Engaged) may reroll the Recovery Check once.

## Characters

Models with a specific name are Characters. Characters cannot be assigned Upgrades when creating your party.

## Monster Factions

Certain Factions do not coexist with civilized Factions. Monster Factions cannot hire Neutral models when creating a party and may only use Neutral Upgrades and Neutral Schemes. Currently, the **Oni Clans** and **Goblin Wartribes** are Monster Factions.
