# Mounted Rules Reference

Mounts are models with the special **Class: Mount**. A Rider can ride a Mount, linking their profile cards together to form a single mounted model.

---

## Mount (Class)
This Class indicates that the model can be mounted.

---

## Rider Requirements
Only a model whose Profile card **lists the name of a specific Mount** may ride that Mount.

---

## Mounting and Dismounting

**Mounting:**
- A model may mount during its **Strategic Phase** if no enemy model has LoS to it, or by declaring **Assist (targeting itself)** during its Active Role.
- When a model mounts, the Mount **appears instantly** on the field.
- A model **cannot mount** if it is in the **Engaged State**.

**Dismounting:**
- A model may dismount during its **Strategic Phase**, **Movement Step**, or by declaring **Dodge**.
- Upon dismounting, the Mount is **instantly removed** from the field.
- A model cannot mount if it is in the Engaged State.
---

## Profile and Attribute Interaction
- When mounted, the Rider's profile is **modified by the Mount's Attributes**:
  - A **+/− value** modifies the Rider's corresponding Attribute.
  - A **flat number** replaces the Rider's corresponding Attribute.
- The Rider gains access to the Mount's **Abilities** and **Inventory**.
- The Mount and Rider are treated as a **single model** while mounted.

> **Tip:** Place the Mount's card under the Rider's profile card to keep important information visible.

---

## HP Interaction and Damage
- If the Mount provides **bonus HP**, that HP is lost first when taking Wounds.
- When bonus HP is reduced to 0, further damage is applied to the **Rider's HP**.
- The Mount does **not** become Incapacitated or Dead when its bonus HP is lost.
- Bonus HP does **not reset** upon dismounting and remounting.

---

## Other Rules
- The mounted Rider assumes the **Mount's Size**, except when resolving effects referencing Size differences (e.g., Stagger, Knockdown) — in those cases, use the **Rider's Size**.
- A mounted Rider **cannot perform Attacks** with Items with **RCH 0**, unless the Weapon Type is Natural (i.e., the Mount's Natural Weapon).
- While mounted, **STK of Weapons cannot be increased** by Abilities.
- If the Rider is hit by an Attack with the **Knockdown** Trait, they are **automatically dismounted**.
- If the Rider becomes **Incapacitated or Dead**, they are automatically dismounted.
- If a mounted model declares a **Dodge**, it may not move during the Resolution Step except to dismount. If it dismounts, replace the model with its dismounted version — no additional movement is made.
- Mounts **cannot become Crouched**, unless stated otherwise.

---

## Inventory and Items
- The Rider may **trade items** with the Mount during the Strategic Phase or while mounted.
- Items can be **stored in the Mount's Inventory**.
- If a **Quest Item** is stored in the Mount's Inventory and the Rider dismounts, the item is placed in **Base Contact** with the dismounted model.

---

## Recruitment and Costs
- When creating a Party, players must pay the **Mount's Recruitment Cost** to include it.
- At the end of a Quest, the Mount's Recruitment Cost is **added to the Rider's** for the purposes of Rewards/Penalties.
