
# Playing the Game

## Game Setup

### Quest Selection

First, select a Quest with the other players. The Quest sets the circumstances, goal, end conditions and other variables.

### Determine the Game Size

The Game Size defines the amount of Recruitment Points each player has for assembling and outfitting their Party. Each model has its own Recruitment Cost.

### Creating a Party

A party consists of a group of models that belong to a single player. Each model belongs to a specific Faction displayed on their Model Profile.

- Choose a Faction, then choose the models to employ.
- A player may only enlist models from the chosen Faction along with Neutral models.
- At least 1 enlisted model must be affiliated with the selected Faction.
- A player may choose the Neutral Faction, in which case they may only enlist models with Neutral affiliation and purchase only Neutral Upgrades.
- The number of enlisted models of the same name must not exceed their Limit.
- A single party may contain up to 10 models.
- Players create their parties in private.

### Leader

After assembling your party, you must assign the status of Leader to one model. As long as the Leader is not Incapacitated or Dead, the player may use Stratagems during their Strategic Phase.

### Upgrades

When creating a party, you may purchase Upgrades and assign them to models during Deployment. Upgrades are purchased with remaining Recruitment Points.

- The combined point value of party members and their Upgrades must not exceed the Game Size.
- A player may purchase only Neutral Upgrades and Upgrades belonging to their chosen Faction.
- Upgrades with the same name may be purchased up to their specified Limit.
- A player may assign up to 1 Upgrade per model.
- A model cannot be assigned more than one Upgrade with the same name.

### Field Setup

The table may be set up according to the players' preferences. Any (wargaming) terrain can be used.

### Format of Play (Optional)

**Rivalry:** Party creation as per core rules.

**Guildsworn:** The player may form a party from any faction regardless of Affiliation. All models have a Limit of 1. Authority Stratagems affect all of your party's models regardless of Affiliation (but not an allied player's party).

## Initiative Check

After the field is set up, all players perform an Initiative Check. Players roll a single die and add the Party Leaders' Morale value to the result. The player with the higher sum wins the initiative and may choose one of the following (the other decision is left to the other player):

- **Turn Order:** Choose who will be the Active Player first (1st Player). Players deploy models in the same order.
- **Deployment Position:** Choose the Deployment zone to place models.

After all players have deployed their models, they reveal their party list to each other.

## Deployment

Players deploy their models fully within their Deployment zones unless specified otherwise.

- Deployment zones are imaginary belts on the field measured from the opposing edges, commonly 8 inches wide (may vary depending on the Quest).
- A model can only be deployed on a surface where at least half of its base fully fits.
- A model may be deployed in the Crouched State, Flying State, or Mounted.
