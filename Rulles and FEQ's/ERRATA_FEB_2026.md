### Page 26: AoE – Circular

**Change / Add:**

The target of the Attack must be fully covered by the template. The AoE also affects models **3" above and below** the surface of the target.

A model cannot be affected by a Circular AoE if there is no LoS between the center of the AoE and the model. If there is obstructing terrain between the center of the AoE and the target, the target may benefit from **Cover**.

If the target's base is too large to be fully covered by the AoE, the model can still be targeted; place the entire AoE template over the target's base. *(Note: You may also hit one additional model in base contact with the large-base target.)*

- Large circular template: d = 120 mm
- Small circular template: d = 70 mm

---

### Page 26: AoE – Spray

**Change to:**

Spray templates are placed with the narrow end in **Base Contact** with the attacking model. The target of the Attack must be fully covered by the template.

The AoE may also affect models up to **5" above or below** the surface on which the attacker is standing *(attacker's choice)*.

A model cannot be affected by a Spray AoE if there is no **LoS** between the attacker and the affected model. If there is obstructing terrain between the attacker and the target, the target may benefit from **Cover**.

If a model is too close to the attacker to be fully covered by the Spray template, it may still be targeted. In this case, both edges of the template must cover the target.

- Large Spray template length: 260 mm
- Small Spray template length: 213 mm

### Page 50: Rules in General Effect

**Add 3 new rules:**

#### RECOVERY CHECK

During its own **Strategic Phase**, an **Incapacitated** model may perform a **T roll**.

- **On a success:** It regains **1 HP** and gains the **Weakened** state.
- **On a failure:** It remains **Incapacitated**.

An Incapacitated model that has a Friendly model in Base Contact, and neither model is Engaged, may reroll the Recovery Check once.

#### CHARACTERS

Models with a specific name are Characters. Characters cannot be assigned Upgrades when creating your party. For a list of all Characters, see the FAQ.

#### MONSTER FACTIONS

Certain Factions are separate from the civilized Factions and do not coexist with them. Monster Factions cannot hire Neutral models when creating a party and may only use Neutral Upgrades and Neutral Schemes. Currently, the **Oni Clans** and **Goblin Wartribes** are Monster Factions.

### Page 60: Dead

**Add/Change (Paragraph 3):**

Unless stated otherwise, it cannot use or benefit from any Skills, Traits, Upgrades, Items, etc.
