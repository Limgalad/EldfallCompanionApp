# Eldfall Chronicles: Quick Reference Summary

## 1. Core Game Elements
- **Models**: Individual entities represented by miniatures/tokens.
- **Attributes (Stats)**:
    - **STA (Stamina)**: AP received per turn.
    - **SPD (Speed)**: Movement distance in inches.
    - **ARM (Armor)**: Natural/artificial protection.
    - **HP (Health Points)**: Wounds a model can withstand.
    - **OFF (Offense)**: Active melee capability.
    - **DEF (Defense)**: Reactive melee capability.
    - **ACC (Accuracy)**: Ranged weapon capability.
    - **INT (Intellect)**: Spellcasting and perception.
    - **AG (Agility)**: Nimbleness and dodging.
    - **T (Toughness)**: Physical strength/endurance.
    - **M (Morale)**: Mental fortitude.
- **Size**: S (25mm), M (32x45mm), L (40x45mm), H (50mm), G (60mm), C (80mm), E (85mm+).

## 2. Dice Rolls (D20)
- **Attribute Roll**: Success if roll ≤ modified attribute.
- **Attack Roll**: Roll dice = STK. Success if roll ≤ modified attribute.
- **Damage Roll**: Roll dice = Hits. Success if roll ≤ (PW - target ARM). Each success = 1 Wound.
- **Critical Hit**: A roll of 1. Reduces target ARM to 0 for that hit and beats all opponent confrontation results. If attribute > 20, a roll of 20 also counts as a Critical Hit.
- **Confrontation**: Simultaneous exchange. Higher rolls negate lower rolls. Winner deals damage.
- **Damage**: PW - target ARM. Each success = 1 Wound.
- **Fall Damage**: Falling distance > SPD/2 requires AG rolls. Failure = 1 Wound.

## 3. Game Sequence
1. **Strategic Phase**: Declare abilities, items, or Leader Stratagems.
2. **Upkeep Phase**: Allocate AP equal to Stamina.
3. **Tactical Phase**: Multiple Activation Sequences.
    - **Activation Step**: Spend 1 AP to activate a model.
    - **Movement Step**: Declare Normal/Special movement.
    - **Reaction Step**: Reactive player declares reactions (Dodge, Attack, etc.).
    - **Action Step**: Activated model declares Normal/Special action.
    - **Resolution Step**: Apply modifiers, perform rolls, apply damage/effects simultaneously.
4. **End Phase**: Resolve effects, discard unspent AP.

## 4. Movement & LoS
- **Normal**: Walk (up to SPD), Run (up to 2x SPD), Idle (rotate only).
- **Special**: Climb (1/2 SPD, vertical), Jump (1/2 SPD distance).
- **Line of Sight (LoS)**: Unobstructed straight line between Hitboxes. Usually from Front arc (180°).
- **Awareness**: Radius = Agility value from base edge.
- **Measuring Modes**: Assisted (pre-measure anything), Standard (limited), Realism (no pre-measuring).

## 5. Actions
- **Normal**: Assist, Attack (Melee/Ranged), Death Blow, Dodge, Interact, Nothing, Perceive, Trade.
- **Special**: Issue a Duel, Ritual, Uncover.
- **Reactions**: Can only be Normal Actions. Requires LoS or target within Awareness.

## 6. States (Common)
- **Engaged**: Within enemy melee reach and LoS. Limits movement to Idle or Walk (towards enemy).
- **Incapacitated**: HP reaches 0. Enters Crouched state. Cannot receive AP or react.
- **Dead**: HP reaches 0 while Incapacitated. Removed from play impacts.
- **Panicked**: Failed Morale roll. Must Run towards nearest field edge.
- **Shrouded**: Represented by token. Secret identity. Can only be targeted by Perceive.
- **Fatigued**: Cannot regain AP during Upkeep.
- **Immobilized**: Cannot perform Movement or Special Actions.
- **Slowed**: Half SPD.
- **Weakened**: Half Toughness, -1 Stamina.

## 7. Magic & Spells
- **Casting**: Uses Intellect (usually). Requires Affinity for the element.
- **Mana Counters**: Mage class can convert AP to Mana. Cumulative across turns.
- **Casting Aura**: Radius = Awareness. Allows targeting without LoS (halved attribute).
- **Summoning Limit**: Total Tier of active summons cannot exceed modified Stamina.
- **Types**: Sorcery (short-term), Healing, Enchantment (buffs/debuffs), Transmutation (permanent), Conjuration (summons).

## 8. Combat Arts & Classes
### Combat Arts
Combat Arts are specialized maneuvers grouped into categories (Archery, Assassination, Berserk, Fencing). Each category has levels (I to V).

- **Archery**: Overdraw (I), Impeding Momentum (II), Staggering Precision (III), Quickdraw (IV).
- **Assassination**: Aiming for the Vitals (I), Cunning Precaution (II), Overwhelming Assault (III), Decisive Blow (IV), Deadly Precision (V).
- **Berserk**: Grapple & Thrash (I), Onslaught (II), Limb Fracture (III), Mortal Wound (IV).
- **Fencing**: Masterful Parry (I), Harsh Riposte (II), Second Wind (III), Grapple & Thrash (IV), Striking the Gaps (V).

### Classes
Specialized roles with unique abilities.
- **Mage/Sorcerer/Wizard**: Magic specialists with rerolls or mana pools.
- **Warrior/Berserker/Soldier**: Combat specialists with stance shifts or damage prevention.
- **Rogue/Assassin/Marksman**: Stealth and precision specialists.
- **Druid/Mystic/Vitalist**: Support and elemental specialists.

## 9. Environments (Common)
- **Aquatic**: Half SPD, AG halved. Lightning damage doubled.
- **Dangerous**: 1 Wound on contact unless AG roll passed.
- **Dark**: LoS limited to Awareness.
- **Difficult**: Half SPD.
- **Forest**: -3 Ranged modifier. Blocks LoS through two areas.
- **Sacred Ground**: Recover 1 HP or remove State.

## 10. Season 1 Quests
1. **Open Hostilities**: Pure skirmish. VP based on enemy recruitment cost.
2. **Awaiting Reinforcements**: Control the central Obelisk.
3. **Treasure Hunt**: Reveal and loot Caches guarded by Sigil spells.
4. **Supply Run**: Loot resources and deposit them in your home Cache. Guarded by Tier III Hostiles.
5. **Secure the Artefact**: Capture the central Arcane Artefact. Halves SPD of carrier.
6. **Magic Stones**: Control 3 Objective Markers. Reset to neutral in Rounds 2 and 4.
